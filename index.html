Advantages of having an outpost at C-Beta

  • Constant monitoring of the rich flora and fauna of our planet
  • Extended military support for protecting the Empire
  • Information and trading hub with the desired neighbours

The outpost included in this project bears a 100 zontar'k x 150 zontar'k area with ten separated levels. Its position at Cardassia Beta takes advantage of our red dwarf's extended energy halo, therefore additional fuel intakes are not necessary. Besides its remarkable vantage point of the planet's surface, it is a perfect intermediary station between Cardassia Prime and the military power of Central Command, also the chain of small military outposts in the frontier.

We determined the best position 3 is high above the northern hemisphere, in an almost geosynchronous orbit.
